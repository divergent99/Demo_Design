FAQ

Common
questions answered.

Still have something on your mind? Contact us →

Is EnrollPath free for students?↓
Yes — EnrollPath is completely free for students. Schools and institutions pay for the platform on your behalf as part of their subscription.
Which boards and streams are supported?↓
EnrollPath supports CBSE, ICSE, and all major State Boards. Science (PCM/PCB), Commerce, and Arts streams are all fully supported with stream-specific college filters.
How are documents verified?↓
Documents are reviewed by your school's assigned teacher/coordinator through the Teacher Dashboard. They can approve, flag, or request re-uploads with a single click. You get notified instantly.
Can parents track without a separate login?↓
Parents have their own dedicated login linked to their child's profile. They see a simplified timeline view — no access to the detailed forms, just progress updates, alerts, and messaging.
How many colleges can I apply to?↓
The default limit is 5 colleges per student. Schools can raise this limit for exceptional students through the teacher dashboard. Application fees for each college are tracked and payable in-platform.
Is my data secure?↓
All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Documents are stored on secure cloud infrastructure with school-level and student-level access controls. We are DPDP Act 2023 compliant.
